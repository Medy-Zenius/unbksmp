How to Stop Nose Bleeding

1.Sit up straight and tip your head slightly forward. Do not tilt your head back. This may cause blood to run down the back of your throat, and you may swallow it.
2.Use your thumb and forefinger to firmly pinch the soft part of your nose shut. You will have to breathe through your mouth.
3.Apply an ice pack to your nose and cheeks. Cold will constrict the blood vessels and help stop the bleeding.
4.Keep pinching for a full 10 minutes.
5.Check to see if your nose is still bleeding. If it is, hold it for 10 more minutes.
6.Put a light coating of a moisturizing ointment or an antiseptic nasal cream inside your nose.
7.Rest quietly for a few hours.
