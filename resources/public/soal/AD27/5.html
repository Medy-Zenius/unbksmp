Dear Della,

I want to write to you today to tell my most unhappy experiences. It happened on a Sunday night 2 weeks ago when I was preparing for my English test. It was 5 p.m. and I still had a lot of lessons to learn before taking that test. Then Aulia and Reta, my best friends, came over. They told me there was a party at Ika's house and they asked me to join them. At that time, I thought that it was the best decision I had ever made because I really had a lot of fun and got relax for a while. When I got home, I was too tired to revise my lessons. I went to bed without hesitation.
The morning after was the most terrible time for me. I hadn't revised my lessons yet, so I did badly on the English test. I got a mark 3 for that test. That was so bad because it was the worst mark I had ever got. I burst to tears right after I received my test back. At this time I really regretted going to Ika's party. If only time could have gone back to the night before!
After school, I went home. When I saw my parents, I felt so ashamed of myself that I couldn't say a word. I promised myself that I would never let anything like this happen. From that point on, I always prepare my lessons carefully before school and revise them deeply before taking tests.
What about you? Have you got any unforgettable experience that you would like to share with me? Write to me soon. I'm looking forward hearing from you.

Yours,
Zelin
