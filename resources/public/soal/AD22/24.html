My First Experience to Ride Motorcycle
One day, when I was ten years old, my father bought an old motorcycle. That was " Honda 75". I think it was small light object and easy to ride it. I persuaded my father to teach me to ride " Honda 75 ". Firstly, my father refused my request but then he taught me how to ride it. With his patience, I could finallly ride it.
One day , when I was alone at home, I intended to try my riding ability. All ran fluently in the beginning, but when I was going back to my home and I must passed through a narrow slippery street, I got nervous. I lost my control and I fell to the ditch.
After that, I told my father about the last accident. I thought my father would be angry and never let me ride again but my father was very proud of me. He just gave me some advices and since that accident, I got my father's permission to ride motorcycle.
