HOW TO MAKE NASI UDUK

Ingredients:
2 cups of rice, 2 cups of coconut milk, 1 tsp salt, 1 pandan leaf, 1 jeruk purut leaf, 1 salam leaf, 1 lemon grass Utensils:
pan, big pot, steamer, rice paddle
Cooking Method:
1.Cook the coconut milk with all the other ingredients except the rice.
2.Meanwhile, wash the rice in another big pot.
3.When the coconut oil comes to a boil, pour it in the pot filled with rice and stir frequently until all the liquid is absorbed.
4.Move the rice to a steamer until it is cooked.
5.Take out the leaves before serving.
