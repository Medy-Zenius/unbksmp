Have you ever visited a place which made you want to stay forever?
I have. Last year, I went to Situ Cileunca. At first, I thought the place was only the same as other places in Bandung. But I was very surprised to find that it was the most beautiful place I've ever visited.
There were fantastic meadows, a lake, and a river. The meadow was very large. In the middle of the meadow there were many cows and sheep. All the animal were fat and healthy because they could easily get some grass in that large meadow. At the edge of the meadow, there was a small river. The water in the river was very cold. But, the sound of the streaming water was good to hear, especialy in the evening.
Not far away from the heard of cattle, there was a lake. The lake wasn't too big, but is ws very beautiful. The water was very clear and clean. We could see many fish in the lake.
The most amazing thing was the water itself. it was not cold although the weather around was extremely freezing.
Unfortunately, I stayed there for just three days because we had go to back to Jakarta. But I have promised myself to come back later.
